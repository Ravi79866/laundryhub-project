# LaundryHub Project

## Overview

LaundryHub is an on-demand laundry service platform MVP designed to simplify the way customers access laundry services through a digital booking and management solution.

The platform connects customers with local laundry service providers, enabling convenient service requests, order tracking, and efficient service management. The project demonstrates product planning, project management practices, and digital transformation concepts applied to a real-world service business model.

## Project Objective

The goal of LaundryHub is to create a scalable digital platform that improves the laundry service experience by:

- Reducing manual booking processes
- Improving communication between customers and service providers
- Providing better order visibility and tracking
- Supporting efficient service operations through digital workflows

## Key Features (MVP)

- Customer service booking system
- Laundry service provider management
- Order status tracking
- Service request management
- Digital customer-provider communication
- Basic dashboard and reporting capabilities
